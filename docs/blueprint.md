# **App Name**: SkyScan

## Core Features:

- Current Weather Display: Show real-time temperature, current weather conditions (e.g., sunny, cloudy, rainy), humidity, and wind speed for the selected or geolocated position.
- Location Search: Allow users to find weather information by entering a city name or ZIP code into a search bar.
- Daily & Hourly Forecasts: Provide a clear view of daily and hourly weather forecasts, including expected temperatures, conditions, and precipitation probability for upcoming days and hours.
- Geolocated Weather: Automatically detect and display the current weather and forecast for the user's geographical location upon loading the application.
- Personalized Weather Insights: Leverage an AI tool to analyze weather conditions and forecasts, generating concise, actionable advice or tips (e.g., 'Perfect for a walk!', 'Don't forget your umbrella today.') tailored to the projected weather.
- Weather Data Integration: Connects to a weather data source, designed to integrate with information available from a platform like yandex.by/pogoda/ru, to retrieve and display up-to-date weather details.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Neon green (#39FF14) reminiscent of the Matrix, symbolizing digital rain and technological prowess.
- Background color: Dark gray or black (#121212) to emulate the dark, digital world of the Matrix.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) to highlight key information and interactive elements, providing a stark contrast against the dark background.
- Headlines: 'Fira Code' (monospace) for a code-like aesthetic, reinforcing the digital theme. Body text: 'Anonymous Pro' (monospace) for excellent readability and a distinct, technical feel.
- Use a set of simplistic, neon green line-art icons to represent various weather conditions, maintaining consistency with the overall aesthetic. Consider incorporating subtle digital glitches or scanline effects.
- Implement a full-screen, immersive design with a clear hierarchy of information. Current weather takes prominence, with other data presented in an organized grid, reminiscent of code interfaces.
- Incorporate digital glitch effects, scanlines, and subtle flickering for data updates, location changes, and forecast views to enhance user experience, creating a sense of immersion in a digital world.
